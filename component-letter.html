Successful Literacy For Your Kids.

Alpha Boost AI is designed to enhance children’s literacy development by leveraging AI-powered tools. It focuses on early letter learning, sequencing, phonetic mapping, and spatial awareness, offering a smart way to practice and learn.

Letter Recognition

The Letter Recognition module enables children to identify, differentiate, and correctly form alphabetic letters. It focuses on distinguishing uppercase and lowercase letters and resolving confusions such as b/d and p/q.

Dataset

2,600 images of handwritten letters (26 uppercase + 26 lowercase) from Grades K–5. Each image was standardized to 224×224 px and augmented with rotation, shear, and brightness variations.

Model Implementation

A fine-tuned EfficientNetB0 model was used as the backbone with transfer learning. Data augmentation improved generalization, and the model achieved 85% test accuracy. Confusable pairs such as b/d and p/q were highlighted for targeted feedback.

Educational Impact

The recognition component strengthens visual perception, aiding letter identification and writing confidence. Misclassified letters trigger personalized tracing exercises within the app.
